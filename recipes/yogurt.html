Yogurt Recipe

Description

Creamy yogurt packed with protein.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups plain greek yogurt
  • 1 tbs brown sugar / honey / maple syrup
  • 1 tbs chia seeds
  • 1 tbs flax seeds
  • 1 tbs hemp seeds
  • 1 tbs fine coconut shavings
  • fruit of choice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients together (defrost frozen fruit first if needed) and stir
