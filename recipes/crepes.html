Crepe Recipe

Description

These delicious crepes are easy to make and packed with protien!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter OR oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar and salt together with whisk
  2. In large measuring cup mix milk, eggs and oil
  3. Slowly add wet ingredients to dry, whisking about 2 cups at a time to prevent clumping
  4. Heat pan on low heat, add a little oil, cook until not runny on uncooked side, then flip
